Design: Safety-Core Fluid Engine

Summary of the architecture and visual goals

ARCHITECTURE_OVERVIEW

1. Data Flow

Webcam -> Optical Flow Shader -> Velocity Texture -> Fluid Solver (Navier-Stokes) -> Density Texture -> Thermal Mapper -> Screen.

2. Components

  • fluidEngine.js: The math-heavy GPGPU solver.
  • FLUID_MATRIX Panel: Controls for Viscosity (thickness), Vorticity (swirls), and Decay (how fast the heat fades).
  • Audio Link: Low frequencies increase fluid pressure; mids/highs add turbulence.

3. Visual Identity

High-contrast gradients (#000 to #900 to #f60). Motion creates trails that look like heat signatures or molten slag.

Success Criteria

  • Fluid reacts instantly to webcam motion.
  • Maintains 60FPS using GPGPU acceleration.
  • Fits seamlessly into the "Dead4rat" industrial tab system.
